OpenAI is expanding its Trusted Access for Cyber (TAC) program to thousands of verified individual defenders and hundreds of teams, introducing GPT-5.4-Cyber — a model specifically fine-tuned with fewer capability restrictions for legitimate cybersecurity work, including binary reverse engineering. The company's cybersecurity strategy is guided by three principles: democratized access through objective identity verification, iterative deployment that scales safeguards alongside model capabilities, and investing in ecosystem resilience through grants, open-source initiatives, and tools like Codex Security which has already helped fix over 3,000 critical vulnerabilities. Looking ahead, OpenAI plans to continue evolving its cyber defenses in lockstep with increasingly capable models, balancing broad access for legitimate defenders with tighter controls on more permissive cyber-specific models.
