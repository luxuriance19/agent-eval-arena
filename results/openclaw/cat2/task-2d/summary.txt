OpenAI is expanding its Trusted Access for Cyber (TAC) program to thousands of verified cybersecurity defenders and introducing GPT-5.4-Cyber, a specialized model fine-tuned for defensive cybersecurity work with fewer capability restrictions. The program is built on three principles: democratized access through automated identity verification, iterative deployment that learns from real-world use, and ecosystem resilience through grants and tools like Codex Security, which has already helped fix over 3,000 critical vulnerabilities. Individual defenders can verify their identity at chatgpt.com/cyber to gain access to models with reduced safeguard friction for legitimate security work, while the highest tiers get access to GPT-5.4-Cyber for advanced defensive workflows including binary reverse engineering.
